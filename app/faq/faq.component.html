What are the conditions stipulated for accounts which are additionally to be treated as Small Account’?

As notified in terms of Govt of India notification dated December 16, 2010, Small Accounts would be subject to the following conditions:

i. Total credits in such accounts should not exceed one lakh rupees in a year.
ii. Maximum balance in the account should not exceed fifty thousand rupees at any time
iii. The total of debits by way of cash withdrawals and transfers will not exceed ten thousand rupees in a month
iv. Foreign remittances can not be credited to Small Accounts without completing normal KYC formalities
v. Small accounts are valid for a period of 12 months initially which may be extended by another 12 months if the person provides proof of having applied for an Officially Valid Document.
vi. Small Accounts can only be opened at CBS linked branches of banks or at such branches where it is possible to manually monitor the fulfilments of the conditions

Can an Individual have any number of 'Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account' in one bank?

No. An individual is eligible to have only one 'Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account' in one bank.

Credit Cards

How long does it take to apply for a Credit Card?

Once we have received all of the required document(s), it takes approximately 9 days to approve your credit card.

What are my obligations when I hold a Credit Card?

The minimum payment due every month must be higher than the following (a) 5% of the outstanding balance or (b) the sum total of all instalments billed, interest, fees, other charges, amount you are over limit and 1% of the principal or Rs. 250

Are there and additional charges if I use my Credit Card overseas?

All overseas transactions are levied with a 3.5% transaction fee. This includes VISA/MasterCard charges for the conversion of overseas transactions into India Rs. Please note, transactions at merchant establishments registered overseas attract a 0.80% VISA/MasterCard charge, even if the merchant is located in India. This is included as a part of the transaction amount in your statement.
