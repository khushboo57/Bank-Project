Important
  • 1. Use a strong password. Choose passwords that are difficult for others to guess and use a different password for each of your online accounts.
  • 2. Change your passwords frequently. You can do this quickly and easily by logging in and visiting the Account Services tab, and then clicking on "Change My Profile."
  • 3. Act quickly if you suspect fraud. If you believe someone is trying to commit fraud and/or if you think you may have provided personal or account information in response to a fraudulent email or website, report the incident immediately, change your passwords and monitor your account activity frequently. If you have questions or need assistance, please call the Telephone Banking Center at 1.888.i bank mb (1.888.422.6562).
